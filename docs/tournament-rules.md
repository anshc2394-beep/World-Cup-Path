# Tournament rules

## Group stage

Twelve groups contain four teams and six matches. Completed matches award three points for a win and one for a draw. Partial scores are ignored until both values exist.

This version orders teams by points, goal difference, goals scored, wins, seed rating, and team name. FIFA's complete head-to-head, team-conduct/fair-play, and drawing-of-lots chain is a documented future enhancement.

## Best third-place teams

The third-ranked team from each group enters a cross-group table using the same statistical ordering. The top eight qualify. Before all 72 scores exist, statuses and the bracket are explicitly marked projected.

## Knockout stage

The fixed Round-of-32 matches and progression follow FIFA matches 73–104. The third-place teams are assigned to group winners A, B, D, E, G, I, K, and L through the 495-row Annex C table. The checked-in artifact is versioned and checksummed, and malformed data produces a developer-facing error instead of an improvised bracket.
